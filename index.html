Description

kitttin is a high-level boss encountered after completing the Master Clue Trail. She is known for her skin-suit disguise, unpredictable attack patterns, and ability to switch between affectionate roasting and devastating meme-based attacks.

Mechanics & Attacks

Behaviour: kitttin remains dormant until disturbed; upon awakening she cycles between affectionate and aggressive phases.

  • Bite — Close-range attack with variable damage.
  • Affectionate Roasting — Non-lethal, drains morale.
  • Meme Barrage — Stuns with pure confusion.
