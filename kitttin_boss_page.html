Description

kitttin is a high-level boss encountered after completing the Master Clue Trail. She is known for her skin-suit disguise, unpredictable attack patterns, and ability to switch between affectionate roasting and devastating meme-based attacks.

Mechanics & Attacks

Behaviour: kitttin remains dormant until disturbed; upon awakening she cycles through affectionate and aggressive phases. She may attempt to distract opponents with cuddles or sarcasm.

Attacks:

  • Bite — A swift close-range attack that deals variable damage.
  • Affectionate Roasting — Non-lethal, drains morale (flavour effect).
  • Meme Barrage — Special attack that can stun targets with pure bewilderment.
